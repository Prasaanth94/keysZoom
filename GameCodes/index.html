Game Instructions About

KeysZoom

KeyZoom draws inspiration from beloved games of the 2000s, where its primary aim is to recreate the joy and friendly competition among friends. The essence of the game lies in determining who reigns supreme by achieving the highest score. How is this accomplished? Through the simple action of pressing a designated set of keys. Sounds straightforward, doesn't it? Yet, it's far from easy. KeyZoom challenges your agility and precision, putting your reaction speed and hand-eye coordination to the test.

Instructions

  1. This game has a 30secs timer, when the timer hits 0 the game will stop
  2. Goal of the game is to rake up as many points as you can before the timer stops

    3. How to score points?

  3. 5 letters will pop up on the screen, player has to hit the corresponding key on your keyboard to Score Points
  4. Score will increase depending on Points and multipliers will be added according to the Combos hit
  5. Combos occur when a player continuously hits corresponding key
  6. If a wrong key is hit, Score will be deducted based on Points and Combo will be resetted to 0
  7. As the game progresses, the difficulty increases and so will the points
  8. Higer difficulty entails more letters popping up and more keys to press
